I watch how she engages  
With the world  
With so much  
Respect and love  
And a fierceness to her   
Kindness that is   
Inspirational   
She is a force of goodness   
Unleashed upon all  
Lucky enough to be in   
Her path  
   
Randy Roberts March 30, 2025
