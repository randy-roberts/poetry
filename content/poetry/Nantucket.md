title: Nantucket
---
Someone commented on my poetry  
Saying it was odd, lacking structure,  
Without rhyme   
So for them:  
  
There was a young man from Nantucket  
Who was grinding up chum in a bucket  
When it was ground   
He flung it around  
Yelling fuck it, you fuckity fuck twit!
