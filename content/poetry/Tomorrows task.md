Tomorrows task  
Is hard  
And fraught   
With risks  
  
You talk with  
Me about  
The pros  
And cons  
There is no  
Easy answer   
  
You bravely   
Decide and  
Prepare to  
Face the task  
  
I get to be  
A part of how   
You choose  
To live   
This Life   
  
Lucky me  
  
Randy Roberts October 23, 2024
