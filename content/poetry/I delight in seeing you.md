Twice today  
I sat next to you as  
You excitedly talked  
With friends  
And neighbors about the  
Wonderful time you had with  
Family this last weekend  
I delight in seeing you recount  
The joys of time spent with  
Loved ones    
  
Randy Roberts May 9, 2025
