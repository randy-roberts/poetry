You moved so  
Much easier  
Today  
  
I am so happy   
For you  
  
And yet I   
Dread the   
Reality that   
Our lives are  
So short   
  
So I keep  
Coming back  
To this moment   
Watching you  
Being happy  
And it is   
Enough   
  
Randy Roberts October 21, 2024
