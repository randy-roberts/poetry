Cicadas singing   
Full moon rising  
Tree frogs join the chorus  
Fireflies lift off blinking   
  
We hold hands  
Watching the moonrise   
Hearing the song  
Flickering signals  
Soliciting our smiles  
  
Slowly strolling    
Diminutive distance  
Bungalow beckons  
Slumber soon  
   
Randy Roberts 2025-07-09
