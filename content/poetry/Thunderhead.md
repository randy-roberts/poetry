title: Thunderhead
---
Stunning vision  
Huge thunderhead   
Miles high 
And many miles away  
Lit bright white   
Turns pink  
Red  
Orange  
Rust  
And fades to  
Purple  
Gray  
No longer visible  
The NWS radar  
Indicates it still there  
Creeping across the landscape 
Heading our way   
  
Randy Roberts 2025-07-08
