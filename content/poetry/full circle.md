In the current struggle  
With home grown authoritarians  
I am buoyed by  
The wisdom and   
Hard won experience   
Heard in a phone call from  
Half a continent away  
My son telling his dad how it is  
A delight full circle moment  
  
Randy Roberts February 25, 2025
