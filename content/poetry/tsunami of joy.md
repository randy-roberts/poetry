Our very grown son  
Hugs his mom  
The ferocity of their   
Love for each other  
Causing a tsunami of joy  
Traveling outward  
Most unaware of the source  
But each undefended to  
The tenderness it imparts  
  
Randy Roberts May 2, 2025
