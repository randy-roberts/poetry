I enjoy the  
Meetings with  
Passionate smart  
People   
My work is pretty  
Awesome   
  
I adore the   
Time spent with   
Family   
I appreciate some  
Loved ones  
Stopping to visit   
With us today  
  
I light up watching how  
She enjoys the  
People she loves  
Sitting in her   
Presence   
Her greatest joys   
Are all people she   
Loves  
  
And I am happy to  
Be one  
  
Randy Roberts November 1, 2024
