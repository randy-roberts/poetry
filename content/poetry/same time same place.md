I enjoyed   
Watching the   
Horizon at   
Sunset  
  
Hoping to see   
A comet  
That was hidden   
By the clouds   
  
Chatting with  
You  
Enjoying the   
Moment   
  
The simple  
Joy of   
Existing in  
The same time   
And place as  
You  
  
Randy Roberts October 9, 2024
