I watch you  
Your body in pain  
Unable to eat  
Drinking very little  
I watch you  
  
I feel for you   
You clearly hurt  
You do not feel well  
  
Watching you struggle   
Is my struggle   
How do I support you  
In this place  
You do not want to be  
  
I don't know  
But I keep trying  
  
Randy Roberts September 15, 2024
